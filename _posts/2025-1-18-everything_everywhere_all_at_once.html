--- author: Golden_Wither title: Everything Everywhere All at Once tags: reviews movies ---

Everything Everywhere All at Once has got to be one of the best movies I've seen in a long time. In fact, after watching it a few days ago I'd say it's easily made it into my top 3 movies, only rivaled by Inception and Interstellar. The pacing is great, the storytelling is phenomenal, the music is incredible, and the overall message is profound.

In a nutshell, for me this movie expresses how in the infinite sea of branching timelines that make up our universe, we are utterly and truly insignificant. Once we realize this, however, we have an important choice to make. On one hand, we can choose to seek instant gratification and completely screw everything up, since it won't matter anyways, or we can choose to live our best life at every moment, always trying to make the world a little better. In the grand scheme of things, it's really up to each one of us what choices, and therefore what consequences, make up our life.

Why focus on the bad when we can do good?

Movies with depth like this are hard to come by, so if you haven't seen it, I would highly recommend it.
