     I remember like it was yesterday, the fall of 2018

  Hopeful,         Fun, Spooky,       And gone.

 A time of interest that has long since faded with betrayal and fear.

 But that time was spent  exploring,  and these new, dark horizons

    welcome me with comfort as I see them once more.

            I want to remember.

           I want to have fun again.

          Happy  Spooky  Season.
