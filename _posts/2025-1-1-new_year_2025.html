At long last, the blog is finally ready, just in time for the new year!

This year is extra special because 2025 is a perfect square, as well as the sum of the first 9 cubic numbers!

2025 is 452. The last year to be a perfect square was 1936, which is 442. That was 89 years ago! The next perfect square year will be 91 years from now in 2116, which is 462.

2025 is also the sum of the first 9 cubic numbers, which means 13 + 23 + 33 + 43 + 53 + 63 + 73 + 83 + 93 = 2025. The last time this happened was 729 years ago in the year 1296, which is the sum of the first 8 cubic numbers. The next time will be for the sum of the first 10 cubic numbers in 3025, which is 1000 years from now!
